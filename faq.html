Frequently Asked Questions

Tracking & Streaks

How is my streak calculated?

Your streak automatically increases each day at midnight. The app tracks both your current and highest streaks.

What are Daily Commitments?

Daily Commitments allow you to actively recommit to your sobriety each day. When you make your commitment, you'll receive a motivational quote that changes every day.

What happens if I need to reset?

If you need to reset your streak, use the Reset button in the Tracker tab. You can hide this button in Settings if preferred. Your previous streaks are kept in history.

Journal & Data

Is my journal private?

Yes, your journal is completely private. All entries are stored locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

How do I back up my data?

You can create manual backups in Settings > Backup & Restore. If you enable iCloud Backup in your iOS settings, your app data will be included in those system backups like any other app.

Subscription

What is SuperSober+?

SuperSober+ is a premium subscription that unlocks ClarityKit, a collection of additional tools and features designed to enhance your sobriety journey.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through the iOS Settings app. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
