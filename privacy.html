Privacy Policy

Data Collection

SuperSober does not collect or transmit any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

Your streak data, journal entries, and settings are stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in storage system.

Third-Party Services

SuperSober does not use any third-party analytics services. For in-app purchases, the app uses Apple's StoreKit framework. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple, not by SuperSober.

App Store & Purchases

When making in-app purchases, standard App Store policies apply. Your purchase information is handled according to Apple's privacy policy, not by SuperSober directly.

Notifications

SuperSober uses notifications solely for daily commitment reminders. These notifications:

  • Are processed entirely on your device
  • Never contain sensitive information
  • Are not used for tracking or analytics
  • Can be disabled at any time
  • Are only sent if you haven't made your daily commitment
